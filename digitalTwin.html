Operational Toolbox

Communication Scripts

Requesting Space

"I need some quiet time to reflect on this. I’ll respond fully in [X hours]."

Receiving Criticism

"Thanks — that’s helpful. I’d like a bit of time to process; can I reply with a plan after I reflect?"

Saying No

"I can’t take this on right now. Could we either postpone or find someone else for it?"

Daily Protocol

  • Morning 10m Planning + Pick 1 Micro-win
  • Block 1 90–120m Deep Work (Pomodoro)
  • Midday 20m Break (Walk/Quiet)
  • Block 2 60–90m Deep Work
  • Evening 10m Reflection (3 Wins + 1 Fix)

Immediate Interventions

  • 5-Minute Rule: Commit to just 5 mins to break inertia.
  • Emotion Pause: Breathe → Write 3 options → Pick smallest.
  • Time-Box Research: Max 2x 90min blocks per idea.
